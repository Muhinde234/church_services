The power of prayer

Prayer serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness with the universe and with each other. It is a practice that fosters empathy, compassion, and a sense of shared humanity. Through prayer, we acknowledge our dependence on something greater than ourselves, humbly surrendering to the mysteries of existence.
